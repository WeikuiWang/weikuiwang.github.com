Welcome My First Job After Graduation

发表于 2016-07-12 更新于 2019-01-17 分类于 Living

![wangweikui_code](Welcome My First Job After Graduation/mynewjob.jpg)
It has been several weeks after leaving my college, which means i have been graduated for some time.I guess everyone of my classmates experiences a lot these days.

As for me, I have started my first full-time job as a Java developer for one week. New environment and people around me make me feel excited and curious.On the other hand,it is a bit lonely for one person like me lives in another city.After leaving my cute friends in campus,it is not easy to make like-minded friends, at least it is true at the beginning of this stage.
After communicating with my friends, I am amazed that many classmates, including my high school classmates, want to do some uncontrolled jobs,like selling closhes on Internet by themselves.They don’t want to be restrained by other people in terms of working time and position.Of course I understand this thought,maybe every young man has thinking about it.It makes me think of Monkey King that he have to yield to The Buddha and start his new journey after maturity.
I have heared more than one time that university just like our social.But I don’t think it is right as I am feeling it now.In fact,in my opinion,campus just like home,we have brothers and sisters there, and our teachers protect us all the time though they are serious in class.But we have to face countless things that are really hard to solve after entering the big social. In general, we have to use our abilities learnt in school to practice in real environment.
An end means a new start!As time goes by, we need to realize that we should never forget those important moments and people, because we become stronger after meeting them.Fighting!
